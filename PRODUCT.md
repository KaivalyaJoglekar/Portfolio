# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, collaborators, and technical peers reviewing Kaivalya Joglekar's engineering work. They are usually scanning quickly for capability, project depth, and evidence of practical AI, full-stack, backend, and research execution.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio presents Kaivalya's identity, selected work, technical range, publication record, experience, education, and contact paths. Success means a visitor can quickly understand the type of systems Kaivalya builds, trust the craft behind the work, and move toward a project link or direct contact.

## Brand Personality

Precise, cinematic, technical. The site should feel like a high-contrast engineering portfolio with a strong personal signal: serious about systems, visually memorable, and built with enough motion and spatial depth to demonstrate frontend craft.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page patterns, soft cream portfolio templates, identical card grids, decorative gradient text, over-rounded glass panels, and timid monochrome sections that hide the work. Avoid replacing technical clarity with visual noise.

## Design Principles

Show engineering range through interaction, not explanation.
Keep the black, ivory, and copper identity intact unless a surface earns a controlled accent takeover.
Use oversized type and thin rules as structure before reaching for cards.
Make motion feel responsive and intentional, especially on repeated interactions.
Preserve readability and reduced-motion access even when the page becomes visually ambitious.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for readable text. Support reduced motion with gentler opacity/color changes and avoid making essential content depend on animation. Maintain keyboard-reachable links and controls, clear focus behavior, and semantic structure across portfolio surfaces.
